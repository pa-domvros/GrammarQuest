English Grammar Quest

Devised by Panagiotis Domvros, English and Drama Teacher.

Follow the path and complete the challenges to finish the quest! Please answer with full sentences, unless you are choosing from multiple options.

Step 1: Correct the Mistake

Sentence: "He usually go to school by car"

Step 2: Ask a Question

Ask a question for this sentence: "I love reading."

Step 3: Negative Form

Write the previous sentence in the negative form: "I love reading."

Step 4: Correct the Mistake

Correct the mistake: "We plays chess twice a week"

Step 5: Ask a Question

Ask a question about the sentence: "We are going to the theatre tonight" (Where)

Step 6: Choose the Correct Option

Complete the sentence: "They _______ a text now."


Step 7: Correct the Mistake

What's wrong with this question? Correct it: "Does they have breakfast at home?"

Step 8: Complete the Sentence

At the moment, he ______ home. (go)

Step 9: Complete the Sentence

She always _______ TV after dinner. (watch)

Step 10: Complete the Sentence

She _______ at the school canteen. (not/eat/usually)

Step 11: Write the Negative Form

Write the negative form: "He goes to school by bus"

Step 12: Choose the Correct Option

Complete the sentence: "My friend, Maria, ______ very tall."


Step 13: Ask a Question

Ask a question for the answer: "I go to dance classes twice a week" (How often)

Step 14: Write in the Negative

Write the sentence in the negative: "I go to dance classes twice a week"

Step 15: Build a Sentence

Build a sentence with: HE / A / SHOWER / TAKE / BEFORE / BREAKFAST / HAVING

Step 16: Choose the Correct Option

Complete the sentence: "He _______ a book every week."


Step 17: Complete the Sentence

Today they ________ on a project. (work)

Step 18: Rewrite in Plural

Rewrite the sentence using the plural: "He gets up at seven."

Step 19: Complete the Sentence

My brother ________ fish. (not like)

Step 20: Choose the Correct Option

Listen! The baby ________.


Congratulations!

You have completed the English Grammar Quest!
